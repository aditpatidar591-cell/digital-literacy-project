
Cybercrime

Cybercrime refers to illegal activities carried out using computers or the internet. It includes actions such as hacking, identity theft, online fraud, and cyberbullying. With the increasing use of digital platforms, cybercrime has become a serious concern worldwide.

Types of Cybercrime:
1. Hacking – Unauthorized access to systems or data.
2. Phishing – Fraudulent emails or messages to steal sensitive information.
3. Identity Theft – Using someone else's personal information illegally.
4. Cyberbullying – Harassing individuals online.
5. Online Fraud – Scams involving money or fake offers.

Prevention:
To stay safe from cybercrime, users should use strong passwords, avoid clicking on unknown links, keep software updated, and never share personal information online. Awareness and careful usage of the internet are key to preventing cybercrime.
