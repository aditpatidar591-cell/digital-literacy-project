
Task 1 – Infographic

I created an infographic on the topic of digital literacy using Canva. The design highlights key aspects such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and basic email etiquette. I used a simple and structured layout to make the information easy to understand. While creating the infographic, I found it interesting to organize information visually instead of writing long paragraphs. One challenge I faced was selecting the right template and adjusting the content to fit properly within the design. Overall, this task helped me understand how visual content can be used to spread awareness effectively.
