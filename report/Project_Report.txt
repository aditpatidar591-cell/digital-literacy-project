
Task 1 – Infographic

I created an infographic on the topic of digital literacy using Canva. The design highlights key aspects such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and basic email etiquette. I used a simple and structured layout to make the information easy to understand. While creating the infographic, I found it interesting to organize information visually instead of writing long paragraphs. One challenge I faced was selecting the right template and adjusting the content to fit properly within the design. Overall, this task helped me understand how visual content can be used to spread awareness effectively.

Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

In this task, I created and updated my profiles on different digital platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. GitHub is used to manage and showcase coding projects, LinkedIn helps in building a professional network and online presence, and Kaggle is useful for practicing data science and problem-solving skills. Creating these profiles helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional digital identity. In the coming years, I plan to regularly update these platforms with my projects, skills, and achievements to improve my career opportunities.

Task 3 – Platforms

In this task, I explored coding and collaboration platforms. I used HackerRank to solve a basic programming problem, which helped me understand how coding platforms work and how problems are solved online. I also created a Google Form as a digital literacy quiz with multiple-choice and short-answer questions. This activity helped me learn how online tools can be used for surveys, quizzes, and data collection. These platforms are useful for improving technical skills and collaboration.



Task 4 – Email Etiquette

In this task, I learned about proper email etiquette. I wrote a formal email following correct structure, including subject, greeting, body, and closing. I also created an informal email example to understand common mistakes. This helped me understand the importance of professional communication in academic and professional environments.

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I learned about cybercrime and its different types such as hacking, phishing, identity theft, and cyberbullying. I also understood various preventive measures to stay safe online, including using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links. This task helped me become more aware of online risks and how to protect myself in the digital world.
