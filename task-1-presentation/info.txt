Reflection - 
While working on the infographic, I realized how important it is to present information in a clear and visually appealing way. Instead of writing long paragraphs, organizing content into short points and visuals makes it easier to understand. I also learned how to use Canva effectively, including selecting templates, adjusting layouts, and choosing suitable colors. One challenge I faced was fitting all the information properly without making the design look crowded. This task helped me understand that visual communication is a powerful tool for spreading awareness and engaging the audience effectively.
